Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Your privacy matters to us. Rootling is designed to keep your data safe, private, and under your control. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data You Provide

  • Propagation entries â€” plant name, species, method, dates, status, and notes

All of this data is stored locally on your device and, if you opt in, synced via Apple iCloud.

1.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number
  • We do not track your location beyond what you optionally allow for weather features
  • We do not collect analytics, usage data, or behavioral information
  • We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs

1.3 Location Data

If you enable the weather feature, Rootling requests access to your approximate location solely to fetch local weather data from Apple's WeatherKit service. Your location is never stored, transmitted to our servers, or shared with third parties.

1.4 Photo Library Access

Rootling requests access to your photo library only when you choose to add photos to a propagation entry. Photos are stored locally on your device (and optionally in iCloud). We never access, upload, or share your photos.

2. iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync in Settings, your propagation data is synced across your devices using Apple's CloudKit framework. This data is:

  • Stored in your private iCloud container â€” only you can access it
  • Encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
  • Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

We have no access to your iCloud data.

3. In-App Purchases

Rootling offers optional premium features via in-app purchases processed by Apple's App Store. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple using StoreKit 2. We do not collect or store any payment information.

5. Data Storage & Security

All app data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework. When iCloud sync is enabled, data is additionally stored in your private iCloud container with Apple's built-in encryption.

We do not operate any servers. Your data never passes through our infrastructure.

6. Children's Privacy

Rootling does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app is suitable for all ages and does not require any personal data to function.
